# Welcome to Direct Book

When you book your vacation rental directly with us, you avoid extra fees and get the best rates. Our properties in Methuen, Concord, and the Boston area include family-friendly homes with game rooms and waterfront condos on Warner's Pond.

## Why book direct?

- **Lower total cost** — No platform service fees
- **Direct communication** — Talk to your host
- **Same great stays** — Same properties, better prices
